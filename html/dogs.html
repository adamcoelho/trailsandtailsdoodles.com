Our Dogs

Meet the Moms and Dad of our beautiful red mini Goldendoodles and our colorful mini tri-colored Aussiedoodles.

Molly

F1 Aussiedoodle

DOB: June 5, 2018

Molly, our beautiful and colorful Aussiedoodle, is such a kind-hearted female. She has never shown any aggression to other dogs or my grandchildren of all ages! Her mothering instincts are the best. She has had large litters and is still very capable of keeping them all clean and fed.

Her puppies will have wavy to curly coats and will come in an assortment of colors. Mated to Rio, her puppy's adult weight will be approximately 30-35 lbs.

Rio

AKC Moyen Poodle

DOB: June 5, 2017

Rio is our very red Moyen Poodle that will be the sire for our litters. He is charming and has an easy-going personality! Rio weighs 28 pounds, is very smart, and lives to please. He passes his great disposition and amazing temperament on to his puppies. He has a beautiful deep red coat.

Rio has been genetically screened for any defects and is routinely checked by a licensed veternarian at West Vet in Boise, Idaho. You are welcome to ask to see the results, since we do not post this sensitive information on the Internet.

Willow

F1 Goldendoodle

DOB: October 14, 2018

Willow is our 36 pound mini F1 Goldendoodle. Her personality is kind and lovable, she is very obedient and loves her tummy rubs. She will produce mini F1B Goodlendoodles, sired by Rio with an average adult weight of 30-35 pounds.

Willow has been genetically screened for any defects and is routinely checked by a licensed veternarian at West Vet in Boise, Idaho. You are welcome to ask to see the results, since we do not post this sensitive information on the Internet.

Doodle Generations

F1 Generations

A First Generation (F1) Goldendoodle is a Golden Retriever crossed with a Poodle (50% Poodle, 50% Golden Retriever). F1 puppies have a low to non-shedding coat. F1 Goldendoodles are usually tolerable for people with mild allergies. People that are allergic to animals are often allergic to the dander (dead skin that is continually shed). No studies have proven that any dog is 100% hypo-allergenic. We generally see more of the Retriever personality in these puppies than in the F1B puppies.

We are not raising F1 puppies at this time.

F1B Generations

An F1B Goldendoodle is the result of breeding an F1 Goldendoodle with a Poodle. F1B puppies are 75% Poodle and 25% Retriever, which makes this generation the most hypo-allergenic of all the Goldendoodle generations we breed. The F1B Goldendoodles have beautiful wavy to curly plush coats.

Willow is our F1 Goldendoodle and Molly is our F1 Aussiedoodle. Willow and Molly are our F1 breeding females, who are mated to my red Moyen Poodle stud, Rio. Their offspring are F1B Goldendoodles and F1B Aussiedoodles, both of which typically have either soft wavy coats or soft curly coats.

Hybrid Vigor

When two unrelated breeds of dogs are mated, the first generation of puppies are referred to as hybrids, thus Willow is a hybrid. Hybrid puppies have what is called hybrid vigor and have less health problems than either purebred parent.

Both Willow and Molly have hybrid vigor; and therefore, will not pass any health problems onto their offspring. All three of my dogs have been health tested by EMBARK and are RISK AND CARRIER FREE of any disease mutations.
