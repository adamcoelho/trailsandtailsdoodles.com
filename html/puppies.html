Trails & Tails Doodles

We breed Goldendoodle and Aussiedoodle puppies from superior quality lines. Our puppies make great family pets due to their non-shedding coats and their friendly out-going personalities. Selection priority is based on deposit order and selections are made just prior to pick-up date. Notifications will be sent the week before the puppies leave my home. Families traveling to my home can chose their puppies in-person and those who are having their puppies shipped can choose using Skype, Zoom, or Facetime.
