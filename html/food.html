Life's Abundance Pet Food

We love and swear by this food!

Trails & Tails Doodle puppies are weaned to a premium food that is formulated by a Veterinarian - Life's Abundance®. We love that it is delivered fresh to our door and is never sitting on a shelf for a year or in a hot warehouse. We love and swear by this food! It is always fresh, never recalled and our dogs have done great on it for many years. Choose Life's Abundance® for your dog, a recipe for a better life.

Food We Feed & Recommend

Every dog at Trails & Tails Doodles eats Life's Abundance®. It's an all life stage food; the puppies go home eating it and we highly recommend you continue feeding it.

Our puppies eat the Small/Medium Breed Puppy food. Switching a puppy's food that has just come home is a guaranteed upset stomach. If you are going to switch, we recommend waiting for a few weeks, giving your puppy a chance to settle.
